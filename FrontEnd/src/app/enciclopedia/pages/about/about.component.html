About Us

OCEAN IN DANGER is a digital encyclopedia about endangered marine endangered marine species, aimed at the general public.

This encyclopedia was created with the aim of disseminating issues related to these species, such as a description of these species, the location where they are found, etc., as well as news on the subject.

An animal species is considered endangered when its existence and reproduction cannot develop anywhere in the world. In other words, it can never be seen again. This is generally due to the termination of a resource on which each and every species depends, either by human action, such as illegal hunting or indiscriminate felling of trees, or simply by changes in the ecosystem of the species that are the result of fortuitous events, such as climate change.
